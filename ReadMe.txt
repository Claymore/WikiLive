WikiLive is a special IRC client that aims to be a real time Wikipedia watch list.

WikiLive is written by Alexey Bobyakov and it is licensed under the terms of BSD license.

The program uses SmartIrc4Net library written by Mirco Bauer and licensed under the terms
of LGPL. SmartIrc4Net site: http://smartirc4net.meebey.net/jaws/.

WikiLive.ico is based on "Wiki letter w.svg" created by Jarkko Piiroinen and licensed under
the terms of GFDL. See http://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Wiki_letter_w.svg for details.
